Bufke

a CV buffer expander for Adje

Bufke is an expander for Adje, that provides an extra CV buffer to modulate your oscillator’s sound. Put it directly to the right side of an Adje, and it will get the information (such as the number of polyphony channels) from that Adje it needs, so it knows which polyphony channel belongs to which partial. Bufke turns a monophonic CV going into the buffer input into polyphonic CV.

If you wish, Bufke can also follow Adje’s clock or its exact delay time. This can be done via context menu.

The CV buffer works just like in Ad and Adje.

If the parent Adje module resets or randomizes, Bufke does too, simultaneously.

A Bufke can also follow another Bufke to its left side. You can chain arbitrary many Bufkes this way.
